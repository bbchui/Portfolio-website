About Me

Hi I’m Brandon - a native San Franciscan, ex-pharmacy technician and software engineer. After two years of filling prescriptions and living a monotonous routine, I’ve decided to pursue a career to be at the forefront of opportunities that are shaping our future. As a software engineer, I enjoy building web applications from the back-end to the front-end, emphasizing on easy and fluid user experience. Coming previously from a healthcare background, nothing is more satisfying than creating a product that is rich, beautiful, and user-friendly.

Aside from software development, I am a 4-time 9-man volleyball national champion and an assistant volleyball coach at Slainte Volleyball Club. I also enjoy exploring new foods and playing various tabletop games.
